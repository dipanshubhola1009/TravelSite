    OUR TOURS

    You can choose any of the following types of groups that you want to do your trip with (The minimum age to register with us is 14 - and if your age is below 18, you will need to have an adult who is above 21, in your group):


    Small Group Tours

    If you are a small group of 5-10 people,

    who’d like to go on an adventure trip to any of the 4 places (Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore or India), we’ll arrange a trip exclusively for your small group…we will assign a guide to take you if you choose the option; or you can choose to go without a guide. Our team will still be in contact with you and will be glad to guide you through messages or calls when you need anything or have issue with any of the arrangements.

    Large Group Tours

    if you are a large group – that is consisting of more than 10 people, anywhere up to 50 –large college or office groups fall under this category - who’d like to go on an adventure trip to any of the 4 places (Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore or India),

    we’ll arrange a trip for your large group.

    All girls group tours

    We organize tours exclusively for girls. The groups can be small or large – depending on the number of women registered for any specific trip or destination.
    We also have female guides who can go with these groups for the trips where there would be a requirement for this.

    To get the slots in these groups, you may have to register at least 2 weeks in advance – last minute slots will be available in rare cases, depending on cancellations and availability.

    All boys group tours

    We also organize tours exclusively for boys. The groups can be small or large – depending on the number of boys registered for any specific trip or destination.

    To get the slots in these groups, you may have to register at least 2 weeks in advance – last minute slots will be available in rare cases, depending on cancellations and availability.

    Singles

    If you’d like to go on a trip as an individual – and don’t have anyone with you, we can add you to a group of similar people who have registered with us for the same trip you’d like around the same time, or even arrange for a solo tour for you – the costs would depend on the kind of package you decide to go for.

    If you’re a girl, and would like to be placed in an all-girls trip, we can do that too – if we have slots! But you will need to register at least a month in advance to get the slots and complete registration formalities.
